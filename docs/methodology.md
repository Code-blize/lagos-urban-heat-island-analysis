# Methodology

## Overview
This study employs satellite remote sensing and GIS to quantify urban heat island effects in Lagos, Nigeria.

## Data Collection

### Landsat Imagery
- **Satellites:** Landsat 8 (OLI/TIRS), Landsat 9 (OLI-2/TIRS-2)
- **Collection:** Collection 2 Level-2 Surface Reflectance
- **Temporal Coverage:** 
  - Baseline: January 2014
  - Current: January 2024
- **Spatial Resolution:** 30m (multispectral), 100m (thermal, resampled to 30m)
